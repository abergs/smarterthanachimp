Hi Gapminder!

My name is Anders. I'm a student at Hyper Island, in the Digital Data Strategist program.

I built this prototype after being inspired by your Ignorance project. I saw the video with Hans and Ola from 2014 and I was so intrigued to see “one more question” because the facts were SO interesting.

But instead of having people read a report, watch a video and then forget about it or setting time off to read all the content, I think we could distribute it in a more “snackable”, interesting and sustainable way.

By letting people sign up and receive only one question by email daily (Every morning) where they will need to vote to see the answer. This brings a game element, an easy way to learn (or rather unlearn) about the world.

I just came up with this and built the prototype above tonight, so I definitely think the concept could be iterated and I’d love to have that discussion with you. I’m a developer and I know how to build this service – but what I need from you is questions and answers to them.

Would you be interested in collaborating?
